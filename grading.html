TSG Hybrid Grading Scale

Every card is graded by evaluating four key categories: Centering, Corners, Edges, and Surface. These observations are used to determine the final TSG Hybrid Grade.

Grade Name Description
M10 Pristine Mint Virtually flawless. Exceptional eye appeal with no noticeable defects.
M9 Mint Nearly perfect with only microscopic imperfections.
NM8 Near Mint-Mint Sharp card with only minor flaws.
NM7 Near Mint Light wear or small imperfections.
EX6 Excellent-Mint Moderate flaws while maintaining strong presentation.
EX5 Excellent Noticeable wear with multiple visible imperfections.
VG4 Very Good-Excellent Heavy wear but still structurally sound.
VG3 Very Good Significant wear including multiple defects.
G2 Good Heavy wear, creases, corner damage, or major surface issues.
P1 Poor Extensive damage or severe alterations.

How TSG Grades

🎯 Centering

Measured on both the front and back using border ratios.

📐 Corners

Examined for bends, whitening, rounding, and damage.

✂️ Edges

Inspected for chipping, whitening, dents, and rough cuts.

✨ Surface

Checked for scratches, print defects, dents, stains, and fingerprints.
